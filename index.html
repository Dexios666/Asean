Philippines

The Philippines, in Southeast Asia, is a culturally diverse nation with a rich tapestry of traditions and languages. Predominantly Roman Catholic with a significant Muslim minority, it celebrates vibrant festivals, offers diverse cuisine, and showcases intricate arts and crafts. Music and dance are integral, reflecting the nation's warm hospitality and strong family values. Historical Spanish and American colonial influences persist, shaping its unique cultural identity
